FGDRIVER v4.02 - FG_QUIET - STOP CONTINUOUS SYNCHRONOUS SOUND

AX = 009Ah

Notes: The FGDRIVER functions are rearranged with each major release, but their parameters do not change (see #03474 at AX=0000h). This call has no effect if there is no continuous sound playing
