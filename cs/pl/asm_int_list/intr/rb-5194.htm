WinDOS v2.11 - TURN OFF ONLINE MODE

AX = AF02h


Return:
Nothing

Desc: Indicate that the floppy disk may be removed, and that WinDOS should write all buffered sectors out to the floppy disk

See Also: AX=AF00h"WinDOS" - AX=AF01h"WinDOS"
