C&T SuperState BIOS - ENABLE AUTO WAKEUP AND SET TIME AND DATE

AH = 19h
AL = hour in BCD
BH = minutes in BCD
BL = seconds in BCD
CH = year century in BCD (must be 19h)
CL = year low in BCD
DH = month in BCD
DL = date in BCD


Return:
CF clear

Category: Vendor-specific BIOS Extensions - Int 1Fh - C
