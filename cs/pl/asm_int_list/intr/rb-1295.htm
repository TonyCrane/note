VMiX - sys_clrwindow - CLEAR WINDOW

AH = 14h.

STACK:
WORD    top left corner of window (high byte = row, low = col)
WORD    bottom right corner of window (high = row, low = col)


Return:
AX = status (SYS_OK)

Note: Clears window to color set with "sys_setcolors" (AH=18h)
