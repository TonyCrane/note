UNCHAIN - UPDATE PAGE 2 OFFSET

AX = CB02h
BX = offset of page 2


Return:
Nothing

Desc: Inform UNCHAIN of the location of video page 2 in video memory; the default is 3E80h (16000, for 320x200)

See Also: AX=CB00h - AX=CB01h - AX=CB03h - AX=CB04h

Category: Video - Int 10h - U
