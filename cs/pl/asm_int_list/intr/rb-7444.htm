LIM EMS 4.0 - ALLOCATE STANDARD/RAW PAGES

AH = 5Ah
AL = subfunction
00h allocate standard pages
01h allocate raw pages
BX = number of pages to allocate


Return:
DX = handle
AH = status (00h,80h,81h,84h,85h,87h,88h,8Fh) (see #03648)
