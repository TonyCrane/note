VIDEO - Hercules GRAFIX - FILL - FILL AREA

AH = 4Eh
DI = x coordinate of an interior point
BP = y coordinate of an interior point


Return:
Nothing

Desc: Fill a convex polygonal area bounded by a contiguous line of the opposite color with the border color

Note: The first fill makes the figure solid, the second erases it

See Also: AH=4Ah
