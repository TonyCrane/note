SBSIM - PAUSESND - TEMPORARILY PAUSE PLAYBACK ON SELECTED DRIVER

BL = 03h
BH = driver number (01h = FM, 02h=DDBV, 03h=memvoice, 05h=MIDI)


Return:
Nothing
