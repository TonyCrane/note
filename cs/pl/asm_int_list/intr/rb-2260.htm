Gravis Ultra Sound YEA_GUS.EXE - RAMP VOLUME

AX = 6909h
BL = voice number (00h-1Fh)
BH = looping type (0 = none, 1 = forward, 2 = back and forth)
CX = starting volume
DX = ending volume
DI:SI = time


Return:
Nothing
