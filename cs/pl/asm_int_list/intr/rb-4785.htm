Microsoft Profiler (PROF.COM/VPROD.386) - STOP PROFILING

AX = 4504h

Notes: Profiling is also turned off by the key combination LeftShift + RightShift. For PROF.COM, this programs the CMOS clock by reading register 0Ch and clearing bit 6 of register 0Bh. It then masks IRQ8.

See Also: AX=4503h - AX=4505h - AX=4506h - AX=4507h
