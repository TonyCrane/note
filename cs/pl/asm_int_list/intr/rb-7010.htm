FGDRIVER v4.02 - FG_KBLAST - GET MOST RECENT SCANCODE PROCESSED

AX = 0067h


Return:
AX = scancode for keypress most recently processed by FastGraph's
low-level keyboard handler, or 0000h if no keys since FG_KBINIT

Note: The FGDRIVER functions are rearranged with each major release, but their parameters do not change (see #03474 at AX=0000h)

See Also: AX=0066h - AX=0068h - AX=0069h
