UNCHAIN - UPDATE PAGE 4 OFFSET

AX = CB04h
BX = offset of page 4


Return:
Nothing

Desc: Inform UNCHAIN of the location of video page 4 in video memory; the default is BB80h (48000, for 320x200)

See Also: AX=CB01h - AX=CB02h - AX=CB03h - AX=CB05h

Category: Video - Int 10h - U
