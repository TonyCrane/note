HP Vectra EX-BIOS - F_IO_CTRL SF_CLIPPING_ON - ENABLE CLIPPING

AX = 040Eh
BP = driver ID (see #03741) for V_LPOINTER, V_LTOUCH, V_LTABLET


Return:
AH = status (see #03744)
BP,DS destroyed

Desc: Causes the physical device's motion to be clipped to prevent overflow or underflow of the logical position
