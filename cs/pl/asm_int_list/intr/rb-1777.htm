HUNTER 16 - SET TEMPORARY SHIFT

AH = 20h
AL = shift status (see #00590)

Notes: The Husky Hunter 16 is an 8088-based ruggedized laptop. Other family members are the Husky Hunter, Husky Hunter 16/80, and Husky Hawk.. The user can override the specified settings by pressing the keys

Bit(s)  Description     (Table 00590)
4      Scroll Lock on
5      Num Lock on
6      Caps Lock on
