HUNTER 16 - GET ORACLE GT POWER LEVEL

AH = 73h.

AL = drive (0:
A:, 1: B:)


Return:
AH = status
00h successful
FFh Oracle GT drive not connected
AL = power level in percent of maximum (0..100)

Desc: Get the power remaining in the Oracle GT batteries

See Also: AH=61h

Category: Vendor-specific BIOS Extensions - Int 15h - H
