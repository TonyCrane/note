VIDEO - Compaq Extensions - CHECK WHETHER VIDEO REVERSED

AX = BF0Bh


Return:
CF clear
AX = state (0000h normal video, 0001h reverse video)

Note: Supported by at least the QVision board and the LTE Lite ROM BIOS

See Also: AX=BF09h - AX=BF0Ah

Category: Vendor-specific BIOS Extensions - Int 10h - V
