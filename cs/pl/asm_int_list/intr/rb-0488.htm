AX = BF0Ah


Return:
CF clear

Notes: Clears bit 6 of port 03CEh index 81h; in some cases also sets index 84h to 80h and index 83h to FBh. Supported by at least the QVision board and the LTE Lite ROM BIOS

See Also: AX=BF09h - AX=BF0Bh
