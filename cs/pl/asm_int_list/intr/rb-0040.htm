HP 95LX - SLEEP/WAKEUP

Desc: This interrupt is called just before going into light or deep (shutdown) sleep and just after returning from light or deep sleep

See Also: INT 0B"HP 95LX" - INT 15/AX=4DD4h - INT 15/AH=4Eh
