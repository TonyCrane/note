HP Vectra EX-BIOS - F_SND_CLICK_DISABLE - TURN OFF KEYCLICKS

AH = 32h
BP = 0012h (driver ID of V_SYSTEM)


Return:
AH = status (see #03744)
BP,DS destroyed

Desc: Disables keyclicks and flushes any pending keyclicks
