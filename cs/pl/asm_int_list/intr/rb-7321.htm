IBMSND driver, DIGPAK - REPORT CURRENT SAMPLE ADDRESS

AX = 068Dh


Return:
AX = current playback address

Desc: Determine what point in the audio data the playback has reached, for synchronization with video or animation effects

Notes: This function applies to background playback only. The reported address may be an approximation rather than the exact address

See Also: AX=068Ch - AX=0691h

Category: Sound/Speech - Int 66h - I
