TelAPI - telreset - RESET ALL CONNECTIONS

AH = E5h


Return:
AX = status (0000h,other) (see also #00397)
FFFFh unable to reset

Desc: Close all sessions and reset TelAPI to defaults

See Also: AH=E1h - AH=E6h - AX=FF00h
