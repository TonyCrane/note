FGDRIVER v4.02 - FG_KBINIT - ENABLE/DISABLE LOW-LEVEL KBD HANDLER

AX = 0066h
BX = new state (0000h disabled, 0001h enabled)

Notes: The FGDRIVER functions are rearranged with each major release, but their parameters do not change (see #03474 at AX=0000h). When the low-level handler is enabled, "FG_GETKEY", "FG_INTKEY", "FG_WAITKEY", and all other functions calling DOS or BIOS keyboard services become unavailable

See Also: AX=0067h - AX=0068h - AX=0069h
