UNCHAIN - UPDATE PAGE 1 OFFSET

AX = CB01h
BX = offset of page 1


Return:
Nothing

Desc: Inform UNCHAIN of the location of video page 1 in video memory; the default is 0000h

See Also: AX=CB02h - AX=CB03h - AX=CB04h - AX=CBFFh

Category: Video - Int 10h - U
